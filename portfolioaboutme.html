About me

 Astra is a Filipino/Chinese designer and illustrator based in Las Vegas, Nevada. They primarily work in digital illustration—particularly for character design and background painting—but they are also well-versed in other design fields, such as for poster work and editorial layouts. They also have some experience with 2D animation and sculpture.

 Being born in the Philippines but growing up in the States, Astra has a unique perspective of culture from a third culture Asian American perspective. They enjoy exploring the diversity of culture in their communities, including those of other people of color and of the LGBTQ+ community. Alongside this, Astra is a big fan of fantasy and storytelling. They are a TTRPG player and enjoys indie animation and video games, with a large part of their creative inspiration being taken from media they enjoy.

 Astra takes much pride in highlighting emotional dynamics in their work, drawing viewers into the narratives they build and the stories they tell through their art.
