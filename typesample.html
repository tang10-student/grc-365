Rooftop Dog

 Writing and artwork by Astra Tan.

They keep me chained up, like a dog with a muzzle. As if I’ll bite just because I can.

What they fail to realize is that my teeth aren’t weapons—only tools. I have my claws and my wits and my bark still to use. If I am chained to anything, it is to this game, and aren’t we all at the end of its leash? And since when have dogs ever attacked out of malice?

I don’t bite to kill. I bite to win.

So I’ll jump

And fall

And fall

And fall

Into the cold, hard embrace of victory.
